The process

How it works

From listing your item to getting paid — three simple steps.

1

List your item, tell its story

Upload photos and write the real story behind it — why it matters, and to whom it might matter even more.

2

7 days of bidding

People bid with their name and photo visible. If the top bidder doesn't complete the purchase within 7 days, it automatically passes to the next highest bidder.

3

Get paid, automatically split

The sale amount splits instantly — your share, the charity fund, and the platform fee. No manual transfers.

Where the money goes

Every sale, split transparently

The two item types split differently. Sellers can always choose to donate more of their own share.

Story items — used items, letters, photos, keepsakes

Sellerup to 60%
Charity / donation20% minimum
Platform fee20%

Sellers can donate their entire 60% share to charity if they choose. 60% is the maximum they can keep for themselves — not the minimum.

Antique items — old coins, number plates, watches, currency, art

Sellerup to 80%
Charity (optional)20%, or the full amount

Donating is fully optional for antique items — sellers can give 20%, or choose to donate the entire sale.
